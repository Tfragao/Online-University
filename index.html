Courses We Offer

With more than 3000 undergraduates and 1500 graduates studying at our university, we offer abudant study opportunity for study all over the world.

Undergraduate

Undergraduate education at Eduford provides high quality classes with a focus on pratical approach. Students are expected to meet the minimum foundation criteria at first academic years then progress to a more senior year where they are introduced to internship within a company.

Graduate

Graduate education at Eduford provides professional development resources, interschool networking opportunities, and other support for Eduford's master's, doctoral and professional degree students.

Professional Degree

With our professional degree, students are prepared to work in a particular field. Professional degree is offered for undergraduates and graduate students.
